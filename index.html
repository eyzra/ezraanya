The Philosophy

Why this exists

Reflection is that conversation where the beliefs you have held about yourself, life, and God are stress-tested. In an environment free from the noise of your daily life, you are able to think deeply, see clearly, and live truly. Reflections W/ TEA was designed with you in mind and the next generation at heart.

What it is
"A monthly gathering where ambitious people pause long enough to think clearly, challenge assumptions, gain perspective, and leave with practical steps toward meaningful growth."
What it is not
"Not a motivational talk. Not a church service. Not a workshop. It is a teaching — and the teacher has done the work so the room does not have to start from scratch."
The goal
"The goal is not inspiration. The goal is clarity. And clarity changes behaviour."
The name
"TEA is The Ezra Anya — and also the posture. Tea is what you drink when you are actually talking. When the conversation is honest. When the only agenda is to think well together."
The Three Pillars
I
Reflect
Most people move from season to season without stopping to understand what the season meant. Reflections W/ TEA creates the space to slow down and think honestly about what is actually happening in your life — not the performance of it, the reality of it.
II
Learn
Every session is anchored in rigorous thinking — Scripture, research, psychology, philosophy, and the kind of applied wisdom that has survived long enough to be trusted. This is not motivation. It is formation. The kind that stays after the feeling fades.
III
Act
Every session closes with a reorientation, not a to-do list. You leave knowing something you did not know when you arrived, and you know what to do with it. The action is specific. The direction is clear. Nothing is left vague.
What to Expect

Every session delivers three things

  • Ask better questions about your own life
  • Challenge assumptions you did not know you held
  • Think deeper than the content cycle allows
  • See your situation from outside your own head
  • Actionable frameworks, not theories or clichés
  • Mental models grounded in research and lived experience
  • Biblical wisdom applied honestly to real life
  • Leadership lessons from those who actually built things
  • Nobody leaves without a clear next step
  • What needs to change — and the honest reason why
  • How to begin changing it, starting today
  • One decision that shifts the entire trajectory
